About

"I believe a good designer is not just a person who soley makes aesthetically pleasing works; They are also avid listeners, open communicators, and imaginative problem solvers who strive to work cooperatively to complete a common goal."

I started my graphic design career back in 2015. In the beginning, I was designing illustrations and logos for friends and family but quickly realized that in order to expand my clientele and advance my career I needed to adopt a professional attitude and good entrepreneurial habbits. I made it a priority to utilize my free time to scope out and attend events to network and solicit my services, research and learn the industries trends and best practices, and critique and learn from my experiences with clients in order to perfect my skills and business. Over time I expanded to working with start ups and small businesses but was still unsatisfied with only working on a freelance basis. Driven by my desire to continuously trend upward, I enrolled in the University of Pennsylvania's coding bootcamp in the summer of 2018 to learn full stack web development with the goal of advancing my career as a designer. Currently, I am still working as freelance designer producing design assets for web and print media. My goal is to contribute my unique skillset and experience in a more professional and structure setting, as a full time designer and developer.
