Tools & Technology

Our game logic is written in javascript. Our assets were stored into a mongo database. We used socket.io to host our game and allow other users to connect to the host site by way of a mobile devices. We used React to template our pages and used bootstrap and basic css to style them.

Slack and Google drive were also invaluable tools in our assignment allowing us to communicate and share documents when we weren't always in the same space.
Concept
Matt, our groups User experience lead worked with our back end team to produce the early layouts of our application. I proceeded to polish the initial sketches into more polished wireframes. From there our team worked collectively to materialize our mockup in react.
Asset Design

Capturing the feel and aesthetic of the 1980s was an interesting task. For the logo I was inspired by the typography of the eras action films and I modeled the ranked cards around recognizable characters of the era. For the suits I incorpated a color pattern that I felt was fairly synonymous with the time.
