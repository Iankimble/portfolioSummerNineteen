Green Print
Budgeting app that uses the 50/30/20 rule.

Summary
Financial literacy is very important. The goal of greenprint was to create a tool that users could use on the go to generate a very quick and simple budget plan based off of the 50/30/20 rule.

logo process
After some trial and error I found that the letter "G" and its circular shape can be used as a pie graph. Even more convenient is the that it is 3 quarters of a full circle. With a little more imagination I segmented the letter "G" into 3 quarters and used each quarter to represent the 50/30/20 rule.
