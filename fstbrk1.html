Fst Brk
Basketball themed rock, paper, scicors game.

Summary
Learning web development has been one of the most challenging yet rewarding experiences in my life. Admittedly, early on it was a struggle to stay engaged but I was adament on learning, I just needed to find the best way to do it. I found that by building applications that were fun and interesting to me I could learn far better than by just doing the standard assignments.
Basketball has always been a passion of mine. The sports blend of graceful skill, sportsmanship and community has had a profound affect on me and was the driving force inspiring this application.
My goal was to design a turned based basketball game that would encompass the spirit of the game and Fst brk is the first step toward my desire to build a full fledge turned based strategy basketball game.
Tools and Technologies
With the early build I wanted to keep the game logic fairly simple by using basic Html, Css, and Javascript. For the next version I plan on building it with react.js and socket.io similar to the build of 21 Vice project.
Concept
It was really important for me to emphasize the backgrounds to give the design character. Also I wanted to put special emphasis on ensuring the buttons were ergonomically friendly for users.
Asset Design
The character and court designs were inspired by outdoor street basketball. I wanted to evoke the feeling of youth by making the characters and settings uniquely cartoony and colorful.
Challengs and solution
One of the major challenges I faced when building this application was getting the buttons to change from offense to defense when either the user of computer won a round. I resolved this but using the toggle() method that would switch between two sets of buttons when a certain condition was met.
